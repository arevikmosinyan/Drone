Controls:

The directions are decided from the view of Drone:

  • Q and E keys: Rotate the drone left and right
  • Arrow keys or w / a / s / d keys: Move the drone forward / left / back / right
  • Space or + key: Zoom in the drone (going up)
  • Ctrl or - key: Zoom out the drone (landing)

    • To land the Drone press (ctrl or - ) and forward and back arrow keys at the same time:
